If you use sync-server, you must use sync-server version v0.2.0 or later.

- Added support for tags; tags are exported during JSON-export/snapshot, HTML type doesn't.
- Folders can be searched and sorted while saving a link.
- Double-tap on the search icon to open the search bar directly.
- The addresses of selected links can be shared (split by a line).
- Added support for alternate app icons.
